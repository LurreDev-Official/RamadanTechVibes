Calculator for Zakat Fitrah and Zakat Mal.

## Features
- Calculate Zakat Fitrah based on rice price and number of people
- Calculate Zakat Mal (2.5% of wealth above nisab)
- Tabbed interface for different zakat types
- Auto-format numbers for Indonesian Rupiah

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Choose between Zakat Fitrah or Zakat Mal tabs
3. Enter the required values
4. Click calculate to see results

## Formula
- **Zakat Fitrah**: 2.5 kg rice per person
- **Zakat Mal**: 2.5% of wealth if above nisab (85g gold equivalent)
